Reveal the most beautiful ornamental dogs in the world

The dogs with beautiful appearance are always sought and become the pride of their owners. Pet Club will reveal a list of the most beautiful dog breeds in the world voted by prestigious organizations and dog lovers.

Alaska dog (Alaskan Malamute)

Not surprisingly, the Alaskan snow dog is one of the candidates holding the top of the list of beautiful dog names in the world. As an offshoot of the Arctic snow wolves, which underwent crossbreeding with larger dog breeds, Alaska today possesses a brave appearance, incredible toughness and agility. Alaska has a high price, from 9 million for a puppy.

Husky Dog (Siberian Husky)

Also the line of sled dogs with thick fur and majestic appearance, many people confuse the Husky dog ​​with Alaska. However, if you look closely you will notice that Husky has a fierce face and shorter coat, so they look much smaller, even half or 1/3 compared to Alaska. Despite its wild, cold appearance, the snow dog's personality is extremely gentle and friendly, and is even called the "expressive god" by the pet society. Therefore, they are popular with dog owners for both their beauty and cuteness. The price of a baby Husky is about 7 million.

Samoyed Dog

Add a name to show the attraction of snow dogs of Siberia, and also close relatives of Alaska and Husky. The beauty of Sam (Samoyed's close call) comes from the snow-white fur, balanced body and gentle face. Samoyed has adapted better to the tropical climate in Vietnam and has become a pet that anyone wants to own. The price for a purebred Sam is from 8 million.
